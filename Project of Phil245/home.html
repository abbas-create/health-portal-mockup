Welcome to the portal for the Ministry of Health in the State of Kuwait!

This website is designed to provide you with easy access to information about the services and resources offered by the ministry of health in Kuwait. Whether you are a resident or a visitor, we hope that you will find the information you need to stay healthy and informed.

Our website features a range of resources, including information about healthcare facilities, health programs, and public health initiatives. You can also find information about health insurance options, medical research, and health education resources.
