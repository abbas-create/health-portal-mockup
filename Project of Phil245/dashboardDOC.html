General Prescription Guidelines

This section contains basic information and guidelines for writing prescriptions. The purpose of this content is simply to make your page longer so menu scrolling works correctly.


Common Medicines

  • Paracetamol 500mg – Pain / fever
  • Ibuprofen 200mg – Anti-inflammatory
  • Amoxicillin 500mg – Antibiotic
  • Cetirizine 10mg – Allergy relief
  • Vitamin D – Supplement

Dosage Examples

• 1 tablet every 8 hours
• 1 capsule daily after meals
• Ointment twice per day
• Syrup 10ml every 6 hours


Important Notes

- Check patient allergies.
- Write medicine names clearly.
- Avoid confusing abbreviations.
- Ensure dosage & frequency are correct.


Prescriptions must be clear and properly documented. Patients should always understand how to use their medication safely.
